Getting Started
1 · Setup
💱 In Settings, set your spending currency and starting cash balance
👥 Add your travel group under Members
2 · Recording a Transaction
1 Tap Debit for cash or Credit for card
2 Select who spent — tap multiple members to split evenly
3 Enter amount → press Enter to add a note → tap Add Entry
3 · Trips
＋ Tap + in the top strip to create a new trip
··· Tap ··· on a trip to rename, archive, or delete it
